nodejs-poolController Pool : Send Packet

To send a message, input in the box below.

Action:

For Chlorinator packets:
The 98 (checksum) and 16,3 (end of packet) will be added by the app.
Example: 16,2,80,0 => 16,2,80,0,98,16,3

For Intellichlor packets:
Enter packet Dest,Src,etc. The preamble (165,xx,) and checksum will be added by the app.
Example: 16,34,134,2,9,0 (turn off circuit 9) => 165,16,16,34,134,2,9,0,1,120

For Pump packets:
Enter Dest, Src, etc. Preamble (165,0) and checksum will be added by the app.
Example: 96,33,4,1,0 (turn off remote control) => 165,0,96,33,4,1,0,1,43
